About Us

Akina Ozele Farm is a registered farm investment enterprise established in 2025. The farm integrates forestry, livestock and crop production into a sustainable ecosystem that promotes environmental conservation, food security and economic growth.cvvv

Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To design a biodiverse, self-sustaining agricultural ecosystem that produces high-value crops, ethically raised livestock and timber.

🎯 Mission

To build a profitable, self-sustaining agricultural model while improving soil health.

🏆 Overall Goal

To create a closed-loop ecosystem maximizing biodiversity and economic resilience.

Strategic Goals (2026-2031)

  • Convert manure and crop residues into organic compost.
  • Establish agroforestry systems.
  • Implement rainwater harvesting.
  • Increase value addition and packaging.
  • Work toward organic certification.
  • Promote women's participation in agriculture.
